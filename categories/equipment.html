Bags, Lugagge & other Equipment.html This section summarizes the equipment choices of participants. We investigated bicycle types, luggage systems, camping gear, and technical equipment used during long-distance bicycle trips. ------------------------------------ Weight There are two peaks when it comes to weight of bike + luggage. Most people’s bike + luggage weights 45kg. Many more lightweight bikers also carry 35 kg. Our lightest participant has only 18kg, our heaviest 70kg. [ graph: bikebags_weight_plot.png ] ------------------------------------ People who are on the road for longer carry more weight, about 3kg for every time the trip length doubles. However, weight of bike and luggage does only minimally affect the speed of participants (about 0.2 km/day faster for every kg less weight). [ graph: bikebags_daysandkm_plot.png ]
