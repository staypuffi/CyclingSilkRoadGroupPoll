This section summarizes the equipment choices of participants.
We investigated bicycle types, luggage systems, camping gear,
and technical equipment used during long-distance bicycle trips.

---

## Weight

There are two peaks in bicycle + luggage weight.

Most participants carried around **45 kg**.
Another group had lighter setups around **35 kg**.

The lightest setup was **18 kg**, while the heaviest was **70 kg**.


![Bike and luggage weight](/cycling-survey-results/images/bikebags_weight_plot.png)


---

## Weight and trip duration

Participants travelling for longer periods generally carried more weight.

For every doubling of trip duration, bicycle + luggage weight increased by
approximately **3 kg**.

However, weight had only a minor influence on speed.
